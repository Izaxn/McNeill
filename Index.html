Before You Arrive

Policies

  • Deposit. A deposit is required to hold your appointment and is applied to your final total.
  • Cancellations. Please give at least 24 hours notice. Late cancellations may forfeit the deposit.
  • Arrival. A grace period of 10 minutes is held past your start time before the appointment is released.
  • Bare nails. Arrive with clean, product free nails so your full appointment time goes toward your art.
  • Guests. Please come solo to your appointment so McKell can give you full attention.
  • Health first. If you're feeling unwell, please reschedule — your appointment will be waiting.
